☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard
1. Business Problem

Coffee shop businesses deal with high daily transactions, multiple product categories, and varying customer behavior across time and locations.

The main challenges include:

Understanding peak sales hours and customer footfall trends
Identifying top-performing products and categories
Analyzing customer purchasing behavior (size, order patterns)
Tracking location-wise performance
Optimizing operations to increase revenue and efficiency

Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to make data-driven decisions for growth.

2. Goal of the Dashboard

The objective of this dashboard is to:

Provide a complete overview of sales performance
Analyze customer footfall and average spending behavior
Identify best-selling products and categories
Track sales trends by time (hours & weekdays)
Compare performance across store locations
Help stakeholders take strategic decisions to boost sales

5. Key Insights Derived from Dashboard
⏰ Peak sales occur during morning to mid-day hours (8 AM – 11 AM)
📉 Sales gradually decline in the evening, indicating lower demand
🏆 Coffee category contributes the highest share (~38%) of total sales
🥤 Most customers prefer Regular and Large size orders (~60%)
📍 Locations like Hell’s Kitchen and Astoria generate higher revenue
🥇 Barista Espresso is the top-selling product
📅 Weekdays (especially Tuesday & Monday) show higher sales than weekends
💵 Average customer spending is relatively low ($4.72 per person), indicating opportunity for upselling
